Sunny Side Of the Moon

A Tourism Site

Team Members were Rami Chaouki, Dimitri Kizyakov, Ali Nehme, Mitchell Fridman, Matthew Rao and myself.

Originally, Mitchell and I completed the Registration and Booking Forms, complete with Validation and Regex.

Later Matt, Mitchell and I normalized the database and added Ajax to the contact form.

A website for tourists who would like to visit and stay on the moon! We used this project twice; once just for basic HTML CSS and Javascript, and later added a UI for our database in the registration and booking section (MySql and Ajax).
