Vet Clinic

A Management App for a Veterinary Clinic

Team Members were Ling Tao, Kristy Hoffman, and myself.

I created the Database in SQL-Server and hosted it on Azure.

The site was built in C# - WPF.

A desktop app for clinic management - owner and pet profiles, appointments and medication's. I handled Appointments, the database and hosting, profiles and navigation. After a few touch ups I'm hoping to make this downloadable.
